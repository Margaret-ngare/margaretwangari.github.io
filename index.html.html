About

Where business
education meets real field experience

I'm Margaret Wangari — a business operations and marketing consultant based in Nairobi, Kenya. I hold a Bachelor of Commerce (Entrepreneurship & Marketing) from Strathmore University and bring 4+ years of hands-on experience managing teams, running field operations, and building marketing systems that actually work in the Kenyan market.

What makes my approach different: I combine corporate-level strategy with grassroots execution ability. I've managed field teams in community markets, built WhatsApp outreach systems, designed performance tracking tools — and I understand M-Pesa, community dynamics, and the Nairobi SME market from the inside.

"I don't just give you a plan and leave. I observe your business, design your systems, execute with you, and review the results. That's the OPEF method — and it's how real transformation happens."
My Methodology — The OPEF Model
O

Observe

I study your business first. Interviews, process review, competitor analysis. No assumptions.

P

Process

I map your current operations, identify gaps, and design a better system tailored to your context.

E

Execute

We implement — together. Marketing campaigns, SOPs, training, field work. Real deliverables.

F

Feedback

We review results, learn what worked, and iterate. Every engagement improves over time.
